Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to buy R3FI? 

1. Install an add-on Ethereum (ETH) wallet such as MetaMask in your browser.
2. Transfer ETH from an exchange (e.g. Binance) to your MetaMask wallet.
3. Go to Uniswap and click 'trade'.
4. Connect Uniswap to your MetaMask wallet by clicking the icon on the top left.
5. Buy R3FI

Why can't I buy R3FI on Uniswap?

If you have trouble buying on Uniswap, try a round/even number of R3FI (e.g. 40000 not 40000.3554) and increase slippage tolerance to 5% or above.

How do I know my funds are safe? (e.g. from rugpulls, exit scams)
